About our class!

The Girls Who Code is an organization that exposes teenage girls to coding and the computer science world. They aim to fix the gender gap between men and women in the computer science industry.

The Girls Who Code's Summer Immersion Program is a summer program hosted by Girls Who Code every summer in select locations to teach girls in that area how to code for 7 weeks. Through out the various classrooms around the world is full of about 20 girls.

Adobe SF Girls Who Code: The Immersion Program I was accepted to was at Adobe in San Francisco. The teacher and teacher assistants were Erica Prenga, Maya Miller, and Mariette Souppe.
